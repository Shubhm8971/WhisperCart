How WhisperCart Works

  • Step 1: Listen & Understand

    Opt-in to allow WhisperCart to securely listen to your conversations and intelligently detect your shopping intent (e.g., "I need new running shoes under $100").

  • Step 2: Find Products

    Our advanced AI instantly searches for products that precisely match your criteria across various leading online retailers.

  • Step 3: Smart Recommendations

    Receive personalized product suggestions complete with pricing, detailed information, and direct links for a seamless purchase experience.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Intent Detection
  • Instant Product Search & Comparison
  • Personalized Recommendations
  • (Future Feature: AI-powered Deal Negotiation)
